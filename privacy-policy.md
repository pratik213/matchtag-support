# Privacy Policy for Match Tag

**Last updated: July 18, 2026**

Match Tag ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Match Tag macOS application ("the App").

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Account Information
When you register for an account, we collect:
- Your **email address**
- A **hashed password** (we never store your plain-text password)

### Subscription Information
We use Apple's In-App Purchase system to process subscriptions. We do not collect or store your payment details. Apple handles all billing. We receive only a confirmation of your subscription status and expiry date.

### Usage Data
The App processes video files that you select on your own device. We do not upload, access, or store your video files on our servers.

### Password Reset
If you request a password reset, we temporarily store a secure reset token linked to your email address. This token expires after 1 hour.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:
- Create and manage your Match Tag account
- Verify your active subscription status
- Send password reset emails when requested
- Maintain the security of your account

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

---

## 3. Data Storage

Your account data (email, hashed password, subscription status) is stored securely in MongoDB Atlas, hosted on servers in the United States. Data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

| Service | Purpose | Privacy Policy |
|---------|---------|----------------|
| Apple In-App Purchase | Subscription billing | https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/ |
| MongoDB Atlas | Database hosting | https://www.mongodb.com/legal/privacy-policy |
| Render | Backend hosting | https://render.com/privacy |
| Resend | Transactional email (password reset only) | https://resend.com/legal/privacy-policy |

---

## 5. Data Retention

- Your account data is retained for as long as your account is active.
- You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.
- Password reset tokens are automatically deleted after 1 hour.

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

---

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** the personal data we hold about you
- **Correct** inaccurate data
- **Delete** your account and associated data
- **Withdraw consent** at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

---

## 8. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:
- Passwords hashed using bcrypt
- All data transmitted over HTTPS
- JWT-based authentication tokens

---

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
